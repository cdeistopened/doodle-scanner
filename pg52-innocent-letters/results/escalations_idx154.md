4× magnification of the native scan resolves the glyph as ού (cup-shaped upsilon + acute): **Καμπιδούκτωρ**, in both occurrences. Confidence med-high from glyph shape alone; corroborated lexically (the attested Byzantine form in Leo's Tactica, which this very note cites: "ubi semper Καμπιδούκτωρ legitur") and by the Latin context (campiductor). verified_idx154.md already carries the correct reading — no change needed.
